Research Interests

I'm interested in the interface between computer science and economics, where I use both theoretical and empirical approaches to model and understand multi-agent systems such as crowdsourcing, recommender system and conferences. My interests include information elicitation and aggregation, peer prediction, peer grading and reviewing, and their interactions with machine learning.
